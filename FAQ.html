DO YOU SHIP WORLDWIDE ?

Yes we ship worldwide. All you need to do is log into your Account and select the order you want to order.

IMPORT CHARGES ?

International orders, now including the EU, may be subject to customers or import duties and tax charges once they reach their destination country outside of the UK. These charges are payable by the customer.

EU orders: Please note that all orders entering European countries after Brexit are now subject to custom and duty charges which are payable by the customer.

WHAT FORMS OF PAYMENT DO YOU ACCEPT ?

We accept all major payment methods including Visa, Visa Debit, Maestro, MasterCard, Amex, PayPal, Shop Pay, Apple Pay, Amazon Pay, and Google Pay. We take your security very seriously, therefore your details are safe with us.

HOW DO I TAKE CARE OF MY JEWELLERY ?

Silver/gold plated Jewellery:
Keep water, lotions, perfumes and soaps away from silver and gold tone jewellery.

Sterling Silver Plated Jewellery:
Our Sterling Silver Plated products will last longer than our silver/gold tone products but should still be kept away from water, lotions, perfumes and soaps to prolong life.
